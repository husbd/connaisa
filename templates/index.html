What is Connaisa?

How to use Connaisa?


You are both Coner and Conee

more details about Connaisa...

Connaisa is a platform to freely exchange knowledge and skills with people around the world. Users select the subjects they are interested to teach and learn. Then, our system automatically matches the users with the same interests. Coners, the teachers, provide courses; while Conees, the students, take courses. They can directly interact with one another through posts, video chats, instant messages, and public broadcasts.


Matching Coners and Conees

Our matching system automatically suggests the best matches to the users, based on the interests of their choices. Users can also actively search for teaching or learning opportunities via other users’ profile pages. Coners can increase their search performance by sponsoring the platform. Furthermore, Connaisa provides online storage spaces for Coners to store their teaching materials, which can be posted on their personal profile pages (handouts, videos, demos, etc.). Coners decide whether the materials are public or can only be viewed with permission.


Direct Interactions with Other Users

Users can interact with other users: posts, instant messages, video chats, and public broadcasting. First, users can post questions and comments under other users’ profile pages with permission. The owners of the page can leave corresponding answers and replies. Second, with instant messaging, users can directly communicate with other users in words. Third, through our video chat feature, Coners can easily provide Conees with one-on-one lectures, allowing instant interactions. Last, with our public broadcasting feature, Coners can provide one-to-many lectures and have online face-to-face group discussions and activities.


Connaisa Coin System

Connaisa Coins (hereinafter referred as “Coins”) are designed for the transactions between users in the platform. Users gain Coins in two ways - users can earn Coins by providing courses or they can purchase Coins from our platform. Coins can be used to take courses on the platform or cashed out. The exchange rate of USD and Coins will be fixed. We will charge processing fee for every transaction between users.


Goal of Connaisa

Our ultimate goal is to eliminate the inequality of educational resources. We believe that knowledge and skills are sharing economy. We are enthusiastic about creating a world where people share learning resources. Currently, customers need to pay to learn new knowledge and skills. However, with our platform, customers will teach their own expertise in exchange for learning new knowledge and skills. Our goal is to make learning resources costless, freely exchangeable, and publicly accessible. We look forward that one day Connaisa will lead the world towards educational equality.
